1. Click on the TileMapScene
2. Click on the example world game object to open its children
3. Click on the layer you want to start painting in and click the purple grid
   icon at the top-right of the scene view. This will open the TilePalette. 
4. Click on the tile you would like to add and click the paint brush icon.
5. Click in the scene view to paint it. 

Note: Be sure that you are on the correct world layer for the right tile. For 
example, the World Base layer is for grass, the collidable layer is for tree trunks 
and furniture.
