NOTE: If you don't have Riggles Player_Movement script, the test scene will not work properly.
NOTE: The Pickup and StatTestUIManager scripts are for test purposes only. Do not use them in the final implementation of this project.  

1. Use WASD to walk around.
2. The shield incons will give you +5 DEF each. DEF reduces the damage you take. A message in the console will notify you how much DEF you gained.
3. The health icons will give you +25 HP each. A message in the console will notify you how much HP healed.
4. The skull icons will deal 50 damage to you each. A message in the console will notify you how much damage you took. 
5. When your HP drops to 0 a message in the console will notify you that you died.
